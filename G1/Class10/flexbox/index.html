Flex Grow

Flex-grow controls how items expand to fill extra space. Default is 0 (don't grow). Higher values mean the item grows more compared to its siblings.
grow: 0
grow: 1
grow: 2

Flex Shrink

Flex-shrink determines how items shrink when there isn't enough space. Default is 1. Higher values mean the item shrinks more than its siblings.
shrink: 1
shrink: 2
shrink: 3

Flex Basis

Flex-basis sets the initial size of an item before remaining space is distributed. Can be a length (px, em) or auto (uses content size).
auto
100px
200px

Flex Shorthand

The flex property combines grow, shrink and basis in one declaration. Common values: flex: 1 (grow and shrink equally), flex: 2 (grow twice as much), flex: initial (don't grow), flex: none (fixed size).
flex: 1
flex: 2
flex: initial
flex: none
